History

Grace Computer & Internet Corporation (Grace Computer) has been providing expert software skills to our clients since 1998. We are currently located in Houston, Texas. To date, Grace Computer has delivered computer products for the retail, wholesale, import/export, motel/hotel, manufacturing, and educational industries. We are grateful for the continuing opportunities to apply our experience with Internet application, windows programming, hardware integration and training to new possibilities.
1996 Grace Computer is a pioneer in web development, providing powerful tools to help businesses get online. Grace Computer was originally recognized as GC International Corp.
1997 As demand for point of Sale increased, the Grace Computer teams started to integrate point of sale system for restaurants and retail stores, while continue developing web applications for customers.
1998 Grace Computer expanded its software development strategies from Windows-based applications only to include Web-based applications. Grace Computer had developed many applications such as Time Clock, management software for wholesale, management software for food processing company, and fast food restaurants during this time. Grace Computer moved to a larger retail store facility in Houston, Texas.
1999 To meet the needs of web sites customers, Grace Computer web hosting services was formed. Also, Motel management software was developed at that time.
2000 Integration of online merchant accounts with shopping carts for e-commerce web applications.
2001 New technology of DVR surveillance systems became popular. Grace Computer had installed many Digital Surveillance Systems for restaurants, warehouses, and offices since 2001.
2002 Grace Computer services had expanded to include graphics design, printing and publishing. An area map for City of Houston had been released that year. Data was made available in printed format and searchable web format
2003 Grace Computer released a new version of Production Management software this year. Its well-designed database and software functions have helped our customers save countless hours of labor.
2004 Grace Computer purchased a new office building located at 6918 Corporate Drive. Grace Computer training center was formed to provide excellent training services for customers and students.
2005 Grace Computer developed many web-based applications such as Training Manager, Online Order and Inventory Tracking System for a major gas company. Grace Computer not only provided software solutions, but also helped built up database information for communities. Grace Computer and Chinatown Map & Directory have become one of the major data sources for Southwest Houston area.
2006 Grace Computer has been working in development of new software applications and technologies for Internet-based business. “Three in one powerful Easy to update web” feature has been used by www.yehcenter.com. These two web sites attractive a lot of visitors.
