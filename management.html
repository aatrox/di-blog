Management

Grace Feng is a co-founder of Grace Computer & Internet Corporation. she has established many services areas such as Web design, Web hosting, Software development, Graphics Design, Point of Sale (POS) System Solution, Digital Surveillance System, Computer Network Service and Computer Training as well as well equipped a digital meeting room.

We commit to use current information technology to help business owners promote their business successfully, save cost, communication fast and stay competitively in 21st century.

Grace Feng has a passion for being innovation in Internet technology began in 1995. She tries to find a way to market businesses in a way that can reach the whole world even with limited funding. She found that the use of websites is the best tool to accomplish this. She and her husband designed and set up their first web site within 1 week with html code. She then started to sell products from then Internet. Since then she has tried to help others to build up websites and educate her customers on how to use e-mail.

She keeps herself up-to-date on her skills and new developing technology. Her services have expanded to software development, point of sale application, digital surveillance system. She was interviewed by ABC news about her business in 1999. Because of her great reputation in the Chinese community and her desire to make Chinatown a safe and comfortable place for all, she was recently interviewed by Channel 11 news and featured on one of their segments about DVRsystems in the Chinese community. She has also been interviewed and published in the Houston Chronicle as well as many Chinese newspapers. View more

Grace Feng is very active in community affairs. Her desire to put Houston Chinatown on the map led her and her staff to develop the Houston Chinatown Map & Directory. In order to aid those who want to learn more about the area and the businesses of the area. Her staff also helps the City of Houston, and Sharpstown Development Authority to translate their Safety Tips into five languages. Her collaborative approach has built a bridge between the Asian community and other mainstream areas between Houston and other major cities. She uses her high-tech knowledge and skills to devote much energy to the Asian American Business Council (AABC) to promote Chinatown as well.

She has helped to form a new National All American Business Alliance (NAABA) and is one of their board members. The organization helps to create a database of corporations, businesses and governmental agencies that will provide resources to seek business internationally. Through this organization, she has tried to build a bridge between government agencies and businesses. She has a clear vision and focuses on the details to make sure that everything she takes part in is as professional as possible. Grace Feng graduated from McNesse State University, where she received a Masters Degree in Science in 1993.
